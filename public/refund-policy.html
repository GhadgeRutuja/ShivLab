Refund & Cancellation Policy

Effective date: February 4, 2026

Overview

ShivGhadge Lab aims to provide clear and fair policies for cancellations and refunds related to laboratory tests and services.

Appointments & Cancellations

  • If you cancel an appointment at least 24 hours before the scheduled collection time, any prepaid amount will be fully refundable (subject to payment provider fees).
  • Cancellations made within 24 hours may be subject to a partial fee to cover administrative costs.
  • For home collection services, please notify us at least 12 hours in advance to avoid charges.

Refunds for Prepaid Tests

Refunds for prepaid tests are processed after verification. Refunds are issued to the original payment method within 7–14 business days after approval.

Invalid or Inconclusive Tests

If a test result is invalid due to laboratory error (for example, sample contamination in our lab), we will repeat the test at no additional cost or offer a refund, depending on the circumstance.

How to Request a Refund

Contact our support at +91 92261 39378 or email us (if provided) with your order reference and details. We will investigate and respond within 3 business days.
