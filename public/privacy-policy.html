Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 4, 2026

ShivGhadge Lab ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of patients and visitors to our website. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information in connection with diagnostic and laboratory services provided at our Rahuri facility.

Information We Collect

  • Personal identifiers: name, phone number, email, address.
  • Health and medical data necessary for diagnostic testing (test type, sample ID, medical history when provided).
  • Payment and billing details when applicable.
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, pages visited on our site for analytics and site improvement.

How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and manage laboratory services, schedule collections, and deliver test reports via WhatsApp or email.
  • Verify patient identity and maintain accurate records.
  • Improve our services and maintain site security.
  • Comply with applicable laws and regulations.

Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell personal data. We may share information with:

  • Authorized healthcare providers when necessary for patient care.
  • Government or regulatory authorities if required by law.
  • Service providers who help operate our services (e.g., secure messaging providers) under contractual confidentiality obligations.

Data Security

We implement reasonable physical, administrative, and technical safeguards to protect personal and medical data. However, no system is completely secure — if you suspect a data breach involving your information, contact us immediately.

Retention

We retain patient records and test reports as required by applicable law and for clinical continuity. If you have a request to access or delete your personal information, contact us and we will respond according to legal obligations.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data by contacting the lab. Certain records may be retained to comply with legal or clinical requirements.
